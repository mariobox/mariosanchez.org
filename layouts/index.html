Teaching Style

I bring a strong math foundation from Industrial Engineering and an MBA. My teaching experience is rooted in the corporate world. As a product manager, I learned to simplify complex ideas for non-technical audiences—skills I now use in tutoring.

With a background in programming, I also apply computational thinking techniques to make math logical and approachable.

I believe math isn’t inherently difficult; it’s often just taught poorly, with too much focus on memorizing formulas.

In my sessions, students build intuition first, then master methods step by step. My goal is simple: to help students overcome fear, enjoy learning, and succeed in class and exams.
